The Best Books to read as a Christian

My top 3 Books of all-time.

Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyasoki

If you want to be rich, this book should be your daily manual.

Think and Grow Rich by Napoleon Hill

This book is all you need to be stinkingly rich and compete with millionaires from tier one countries.

The Richest Man in Babylon By George S Clason

True richest can only be learned from those who made huge liquid in their lifetime. Don't let anyone cajole you into not believing in real wealth and substance.
